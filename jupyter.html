Project Objective

Project Jupyter lacked in a consistent design theme across the board. There were a dozen different variations of the logo used in inconsistent ways. Two designers and I decided to redefine the brand guidelines to develop a cohesive theme for the organization's future use. We began with creating a new version of the logo following this brand guideline book to standardize all design specifications.

Presentation

We presented this book to the wider Jupyter community and explained each design decision to an audience that consisted of 90% developers and engineers. We were praised and applauded for our work. It was a very rewarding experience to have collaborated with my fellow designers to create a unified and strong visual identity for Jupyter.

This guide now lives in the Jupyter Design Github.
